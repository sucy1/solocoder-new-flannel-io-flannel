## Code of Conduct

Flannel follows the [CNCF Code of Conduct](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md).

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers listed in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) or by emailing conduct@cncf.io.
